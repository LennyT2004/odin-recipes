Smash Burger

Description

A Smash Burger is a culinary delight that embodies simplicity and flavor in perfect harmony. Crafted with precision, this burger starts with a high-quality ground beef patty expertly seasoned and smashed onto a hot griddle. The result is a thin, crispy crust that seals in juiciness. Topped with a melty slice of cheese, fresh lettuce, tomatoes, and condiments, the Smash Burger is a sensory explosion, delivering a rich umami taste with each bite. The marriage of textures, from the crunch of the crust to the tenderness of the beef, creates an unforgettable dining experience, making the Smash Burger a beloved classic in the world of gastronomy.

Ingredients

  • 600g of ground beef
  • 2 tablesoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 slices of thick sliced bacon
  • 1 small yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 burger buns
  • 4 slices of yellow cheddar
  • 2 slices of iceberg lettuce
  • 4 tablespoons of spicy mayonnaise
  • Salt

Instructions

  • With the meat, form 4 balls without flattening them too much
  • Light the Plancha on high heat on one side and low heat on the other
  • Oil the lit side over high heat, place the meatballs and, using a press, flatten as much as possible
  • Cook for about 2 minutes, season with salt then turn
  • Continue cooking for another 2 minutes and set aside over low heat
  • On the high heat side, cook the bacon and brown the onions then, once everything is well cooked, set aside over low heat
  • Grill the buns on the plancha and assemble the burgers in the following order: bottom bun, spicy mayo, slice of lettuce, meatball, cheese, bacon, meatball, cheese, spicy mayo then top bun
